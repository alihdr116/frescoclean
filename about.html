About FrescoClean - Trusted Cleaning Experts in the UK

Our Story

FrescoClean was built with a simple goal — to provide professional cleaning services that people can truly rely on. We noticed that many customers were struggling to find cleaners who were both affordable and consistent in quality.

Starting with a small team of trained cleaners, we gradually expanded across residential and commercial sectors. Today, we serve multiple locations across the UK with a strong reputation for reliability and customer satisfaction.

Our growth is based on trust, hard work, and consistent service quality. Every job we take is handled with care, professionalism, and attention to detail.
